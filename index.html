About PassX

PassX is your personal password manager. Store your credentials securely in your browser using local storage. This project is lightweight, fast, and private. All data stays on your device.

  • Dark mode support
  • Password strength indicator
  • Copy to clipboard functionality
  • Local storage (no cloud dependency)
